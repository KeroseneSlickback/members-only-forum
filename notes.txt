The user comes to the Home page
They're presented with Log In or Sign Up buttons
Log In routes them to the Login Form
Signup routes them to the Signup Form

Log In ports them to the Log In controller that verifies their account with the database
Signup ports them to the Signup controller that creates an account
Both, when done, ports them to the messaging board.

The Messaging Board shows the messages and time stamps, but every user is Anon
Within the Messaging Board, there's a button that enters them into the Members messaging board that shows everything
Within either, they can post messages using a simple form
